Controls



  • WASD to move
  • Arrow keys to move
  • Shift is do enable dash mode
  • Space to use the current item
  • M to mute
  • 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8 for the hotbar slots
  • X to remove all the current items
  • Z to remove one of the current items
  • , to drop one of your current item
  • . to drop all of your current item
  • C to run a command
  • T to trade one item
  • Y to trade all items
  • 9 to save your game (This can be buggy)
  • 0 to load your saved game (This can be buggy)
  • - to Quit the game
  • I to put a item in a chest
  • U to take an item out of a chest
  • G to get unstuck
  • P to pause and unpause the game
  • = to get into your car
  • R to open your GUI
